My Year - Viewing Your Plan

You can get here by pressing the 'House' icon.

This provides a graphical forecast of your annual Plan, based on your inputs. Positive balances are shown in green and negative ones in red.
This will show your target bank balance for each day of the first week.
This will show your target bank balance for each day of the first 4 weeks.
This will display a breakdown of your forecast expenditure for the year.
This gives hints and tips on how you could make changes that will potentially improve your forecast bank balance. You can make changes to frequencies, amounts and dates by pressing those boxes containing a 'Pencil' icon. You can also delete entire lines.
Press 'Save' against each line you have changed or deleted. NB You must press 'Save' at the end of each line you have changed or deleted.

Message alerts about your Plan, if applicable, will appear beneath the graph, showing your Lowest Balance in the year, your First Negative Balance in the year and your End of Year Balance.
